## Navigation Notes
- From The Red Room, going DOWN leads to Great Field
- Great Field south leads to Behind Temple Altar area
- Behind Temple Altar south leads to Temple Square
- From Temple Square south is Market Square
- Bakery is on Main Street, which connects to Market Square
